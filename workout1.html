Workout Routine 1: Home Workout 1

Start lying on a bench with your feet flat on the floor and your back flat against the bench.

Grip the barbell with a wider than shoulder-width grip.

Lower the barbell to your chest, then press it back up to the starting position.

3 sets of 10-12 reps

  • Push-ups

    Start in a plank position with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.

    Lower your body towards the floor until your chest nearly touches the ground.

    Push back up to the starting position.

    3 sets of as many reps as possible
