Fitness Goals

Goal 1: Build Muscle

To build muscle, focus on strength training exercises that target specific muscle groups.

Here's a sample workout routine:

  • Bench Press

    Lie on a bench with your feet flat on the floor and your back flat against the bench.

    Grip the barbell with a wider than shoulder-width grip.

    Lower the barbell to your chest, then press it back up to the starting position.

    3 sets of 8-12 reps

  • Deadlifts

    Stand in front of a barbell with your feet hip-width apart.

    Hinge at your hips and knees to grab the barbell with a wide grip.

    Lift the barbell off the ground, keeping your back straight.

    3 sets of 5-8 reps
